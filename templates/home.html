Guardrails that execute
faster than emotion

Every order you place passes through a rules engine before it reaches the exchange.

Hard 1% Risk Cap

Position size is computed from your stop distance automatically. No trade can ever risk more than 1% of your account — the platform won't allow it.

Automated TP & SL

TP1, TP2 and stop-loss orders are placed on the exchange the instant your entry fills. Partial profit-taking runs on autopilot, even while you sleep.

Trailing Stop Engine

As price moves your way, the stop follows — locking in profit and moving to breakeven after TP1. Winners run; losers get cut mechanically.

Overtrade Protection

A maximum of 4 trades per day and 2 per symbol. When you hit the limit, the platform locks new entries — revenge trading ends here.

Live P&L Dashboard

Real-time positions, wallet balance, realized P&L and a full trade journal with CSV export. Know exactly where you stand, always.

Secure API Connection

Connect with exchange API keys that can trade but never withdraw. Your funds stay on your exchange account — we never hold your money.
